- **Group 1 (idle-reaping mitigation) — n8n-mcp-session-durability.** Pinned the n8n-mcp sidecar's idle-session reap window to `SESSION_TIMEOUT_MINUTES="120"` in `scripts/render-mcp-service-yaml.sh` (n8nmcp env block, after `LOG_LEVEL`). Proven by a local render asserting `SESSION_TIMEOUT_MINUTES=120` inside the `n8nmcp` container; shellcheck-clean. The durable cross-instance store (Layer B) follows in Group 2.
